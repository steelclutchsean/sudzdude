Why SUDZ DUDE

Built around the work,
not the upsell.

01 · Coatings

Ceramic protection that actually lasts.

Hydrophobic beads aren't a trick — they're the result of a properly prepped panel and a real coating cured under controlled conditions. We use professional-grade ceramic, not bottle wax in a fancy box.

  • Multi-year warranty options
  • Hand-leveled for an even, glossy finish
  • Includes annual inspection
02 · Correction

Paint correction done by hand.

Swirls, holograms, and water spots get measured, then leveled with the right pad and compound for your paint's hardness — not a one-size-fits-all approach. The result: clarity you can see in the reflection.

  • Paint depth measurement
  • Test spot before full correction
  • IPA wipedown to confirm finish
03 · Mobile

100% mobile. Your driveway, our shop.

The trailer carries deionized water for a spot-free rinse, generator power, and pro lighting for inspection. The only thing missing from a real shop is the drive across town.

  • Deionized water — no water spots
  • Self-contained power & water
  • Easy online & text scheduling
